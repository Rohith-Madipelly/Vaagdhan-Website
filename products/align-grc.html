Align AI GRC: Navigate the AI Landscape with Confidence

As artificial intelligence becomes integral to business operations, managing its risks, ensuring compliance, and overseeing third-party AI vendors is paramount. Introducing AI-GRC, your compass in the complex world of AI governance and risk management.

What is Align AI-GRC?

AI-GRC is a cutting-edge platform that helps organizations manage governance, risk, and compliance in AI adoption, including third-party AI vendors. Through advanced analytics and assessments, it offers deep insights into AI systems' risk profiles, compliance, and vendor relationships.

Key Features of Align GRC

Discover the essential tools and functionalities that Align GRC offers to streamline your AI governance, manage risks, and ensure compliance, all within a unified platform.

Inventory:

The single source of truth for everything about every AI system across the enterprise.


  • Visibility and accountability

  • Risk tiering

  • Systemic tracking and audibility

Controls

Instantly and proactively manage the risk and returns of your AI investments.

  • Policy enforcement

  • Process management

  • Production controls

Reporting

Generate reports tailored for any stakeholder. Get timely and relevant notifications so you're always in the loop. Publish to your AI Trustcentre and share these reports publicly.

  • Auto Generate Reports

  • AI Public Trust Centre

Third-Party Evaluation

Evaluate and manage vendor risks by ensuring their AI solutions meet governance standards. Continuously monitor third-party AI systems to identify and mitigate potential risks.

  • Ensure third-party AI compliance.

  • Monitor external AI for risks.
