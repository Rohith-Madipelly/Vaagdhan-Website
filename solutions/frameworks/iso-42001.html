Meet the Requirements of ISO 42001

At its core, ISO/IEC 42001 establishes international foundational practices for organizations to develop AI responsibly and effectively, while promoting public trust in AI systems with a standard that can eventually be certified through a third-party audit.

Streamline Your AI Risk Management Process

Comprehensive Strategies for Managing High-Risk AI Use Cases

ISO and IEC set best practices across various domains, like ISO/IEC 27001 in cybersecurity. ISO/IEC 42001 aims to establish a similar benchmark for AI governance, enabling organizations to certify and showcase the trustworthiness of their AI systems.

Our AI governance solution prepares you for ISO 42001 with:

  1. AI Registry and Intake: Centralizes use case inventory and tracks documentation for ISO/IEC 42001 compliance.


  1. Framework: Offers actionable steps for adopting and monitoring compliance with ISO/IEC 42001 and other regulations.


  1. Intelligent Risk Management: Meets ISO 42001 requirements with AI-specific risk scenarios and controls.


  1. Generate audit-ready reports: Generates reports demonstrating adherence to ISO 42001 and other regulatory frameworks.
